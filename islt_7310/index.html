Hi! My name is Sarah Pauley. I enjoy reading, writing, and sharing my love of language with students.

The goal of this website is to demonstrate my creativity and competency as an educator to potential employers. This site functions as a professional portfolio where I reflect on my experiences, achievements, and future goals. The about page summarizes my educational background and links to my resume. The experience page describes my most recent teaching positions. On this page, I reflect on the skills and experience I gained in each position. I also provide pictures and pdfs that demonstrate my achievements in that position. The philosophy page outlines my teaching philosophy. I describe my belief in curiosity, creativity, and effort and explain how my philosophy informs my current practice and future goals.
